Human Centered Design for Social Impact

Berkeley Energy and Sustainable Technologies Lab

September 2014 - September 2015

I studied IDEO.org, IDDS, and Gobee's design approaches and their adaptations of the Human Centered Design Method.

Later in the research, I focused on IDDS and IDIN projects (both technical and non-technical). I studied how the projects implemented HCD, and how they were successsful with their social impact and capacity building initiatives for local communities.

All of my research is used by a BEST Lab's Ph.D student in her dissertation on "Human Centered Design for Social Impact." (Reach out to me and I will get you in touch with her).
